#### Scroll-triggered reveal animations
- `IntersectionObserver` script at bottom of `<body>` pauses all `.fade-in` elements on load
- Elements fade in (`fadeInUp` 0.6s) as they enter the viewport while scrolling
- Threshold: 15% visibility, rootMargin: `-40px` bottom offset
- Above-fold elements animate immediately; all others wait for scroll
- Graceful fallback for browsers without IntersectionObserver support

- `.section-divider` margin reduced from `2.5rem` → `0.75rem` (desktop), `2rem` → `0.5rem` (mobile)
